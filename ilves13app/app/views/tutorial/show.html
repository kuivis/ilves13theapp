Lessons learned

So far you have mastered that:

config/application.coffee configures your project settings.

app/ folder has Steroids benefits, it's a good place for your applications views.

www/ folder is for your other files.

app/ and www/ are brought together as dist/ folder.

http://localhost serves your project files from dist/.

Everything that works in PhoneGap, works in Steroids.
